Fun Fact

The deerstalker hat, a popular symbol of Sherlock Holmes, is never mentioned in the books, but was added by an illustrator.

Welcome to the Sherlock Holmes Fan Site!

Sherlock Holmes is a character created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle in the late 19th century, is one of the most enduring figures in detective literature. Known for his keen observation skills, logical reasoning, and forensic science application, Holmes solves mysteries that baffle Scotland Yard. He resides at 221B Baker Street, London, with his loyal friend Dr. John Watson, who narrates most of the stories. The character's popularity has led to numerous adaptations in film, television, and other media, making Sherlock Holmes a timeless icon in detective fiction.
