About Statistics

The immortal detective made his first appearance in “A Study in Scarlet” in 1887, introducing to the world not only Holmes, but also his faithful friend and companion, Dr. John Watson. Since then, Holmes has transcended the pages of books to become a global cultural icon. With over 25,000 adaptations in various media, Holmes holds the record for being the most portrayed human literary character in history. A key element of Holmes' mythology is his iconic address, 221B Baker Street, in London. Although it is a fictional residence, it has become a pilgrimage site for Sherlock Holmes fans around the world.
